About Us

RICH$LOVE Savings Club is a member-driven community where each person commits to regular savings, supports each other, and benefits from collective growth. We believe in empowerment, transparency and long-term success.

How It Works

  1. Plan: You choose your savings goal — monthly contribution, duration, target amount.
  2. Save: You make your regular contributions and track progress alongside fellow members.
  3. Benefit: Once you reach milestones, you gain access to funds, shared returns, or member-exclusive opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join?
Anyone aged 18 and above, committed to regular contributions and abiding by club rules.
How are funds distributed?
Funds are pooled and allocated based on the plan’s structure. Members benefit collectively and individually.
Is there interest or hidden fees?
We operate with transparency — administrative fee only, no hidden interest. All numbers are clarified upfront.
What happens if I miss a payment?
Late payments affect your progression. We encourage timely contributions for the benefit of all.
